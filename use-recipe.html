Homemade Bacon and Eggs, with Toast!

Notes

  • Time: 5 mins
  • Servings: 2 people
  • Breakfast
  • Meat
  • Bacon
  • Eggs
  • Gluten-Free
  • Dairy-Free

Ingredients

  • Eggs (4)
  • Bacon (2 Pieces)
  • Milk (1 Cup)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter

Pantry

  • Skillet
  • Measuring Cup
  • Mixing Bowl

Directions

  1. Gather your ingredients.
  2. Cook 2 pieces of bacon according to package directions. Once cooked, cut into ~1/2 inch slices.
  3. Crack eggs in a mixing bowl. Add milk, a dash of salt and pepper, and bacon to the bowl. Whisk until the eggs are beaten.
